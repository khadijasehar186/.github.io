Privacy Policy

🔒 Our Commitment to Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website. We are committed to transparency and handling your data with the utmost care.

📋 Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Email address (when subscribing to newsletter)
  • Name (when contacting us)
  • Messages you send through contact forms

Automatically Collected Information

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages you visit
  • Time and date of visit
  • Referring website addresses

🎯 How We Use Your Information

  • To send you newsletters (only if you subscribed)
  • To respond to your questions

🍪 Cookies

We use minimal cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Understand how you use our site

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. However, please note that some features of our site may not function properly without cookies.

This website participates in affiliate marketing programs. This means we may earn commissions on purchases made through our links at no extra cost to you. We only recommend products we genuinely believe in and have researched thoroughly.

These affiliate relationships help support the maintenance and growth of this website, allowing us to continue providing free, valuable content.

✉️ Email Communications

If you subscribe to our newsletter, you can unsubscribe at any time using the link at the bottom of every email. We never share, sell, or rent your email address to third parties. Your inbox is yours, and we respect that.

🛡️ Data Protection

We implement security measures to protect your personal information, including SSL encryption for data transmission. However, no online transmission is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security. We recommend using strong passwords and keeping your login information confidential.

🔗 Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies separately before providing any personal information.

⚖️ Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Ask for data correction or deletion
  • Opt-out of communications
  • Disable cookies
  • Request data portability

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

👶 Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

📅 Policy Updates

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

📞 Contact Us

For privacy questions, requests, or concerns, please use our contact form or email us directly at privacy@themodernguide.com. We typically respond within 2-3 business days.

This policy reflects our commitment to transparency and your privacy rights. We believe in building trust through honest communication about how we handle your data.
